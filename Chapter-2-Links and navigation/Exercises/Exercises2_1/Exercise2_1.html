Overview

"Arcane" follows the story of two iconic League of Legends champions, Vi and Jinx, and their journey through the corrupt and divided city of Piltover and the oppressed underground of Zaun.

Cast

  • Hailee Steinfeld as Vi
  • Ella Purnell as Jinx
  • Kevin Alejandro as Jayce
  • Jason Spisak as Silco
  • JB Blanc as Vander

Reviews
