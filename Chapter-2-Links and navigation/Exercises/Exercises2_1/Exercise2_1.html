Plot

"Arcane" follows the story of two iconic League of Legends champions, Vi and Jinx. Set in the fictional world of Runeterra, the series explores their journey through the corrupt and divided city of Piltover and the oppressed underground of Zaun. As they navigate through the complexities of their surroundings, they uncover deep-rooted secrets and face moral dilemmas that challenge their beliefs and values.

Key Information

Release Date: November 6, 2021
Directors: Christian Linke, Alex Yee
Number of Seasons: 1
Number of Episodes: 9

Set in the vibrant universe of League of Legends, "Arcane" is a visually stunning animated series that premiered on November 6, 2021. Directed by Christian Linke and Alex Yee, the series consists of one season comprising nine episodes, each offering an immersive experience into the rich and diverse world of Runeterra.

Cast & Characters

Vi - Hailee Steinfeld
Jinx - Ella Purnell
Jayce - Kevin Alejandro
Silco - Jason Spisak
Vander - JB Blanc

"Arcane" boasts a talented cast that brings the iconic characters to life. Hailee Steinfeld portrays the determined and passionate Vi, while Ella Purnell embodies the complex and unpredictable Jinx. Kevin Alejandro, Jason Spisak, and JB Blanc deliver captivating performances as Jayce, Silco, and Vander, respectively, adding depth and authenticity to the characters' personalities.
