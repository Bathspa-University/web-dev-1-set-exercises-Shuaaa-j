"Arcane" is an animated series that immerses viewers into the vibrant and complex universe of League of Legends. Set in the city of Piltover and the oppressed underground of Zaun, the series follows the journeys of iconic champions as they navigate through a world filled with intrigue, conflict, and deep-rooted secrets. Experience the excitement, adventure, and emotion as characters forge alliances, confront adversaries, and uncover the truth that lies beneath the surface of their divided society.
