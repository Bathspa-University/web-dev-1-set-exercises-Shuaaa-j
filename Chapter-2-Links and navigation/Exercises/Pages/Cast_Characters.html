Cast & Characters

Vi - Hailee Steinfeld
Jinx - Ella Purnell
Jayce - Kevin Alejandro
Silco - Jason Spisak
Vander - JB Blanc

"Arcane" boasts a talented cast that brings the iconic characters to life. Hailee Steinfeld portrays the determined and passionate Vi, while Ella Purnell embodies the complex and unpredictable Jinx. Kevin Alejandro, Jason Spisak, and JB Blanc deliver captivating performances as Jayce, Silco, and Vander, respectively, adding depth and authenticity to the characters' personalities.

Additionally, "Arcane" features a diverse cast of characters, each with their own unique backgrounds, motivations, and personalities. From the determined and passionate Vi to the complex and unpredictable Jinx, the series explores the intricacies of their relationships and the impact of their choices on the world of Runeterra.
