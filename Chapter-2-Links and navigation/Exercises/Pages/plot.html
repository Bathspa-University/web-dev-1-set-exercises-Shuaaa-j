Plot

"Arcane" follows the story of two iconic League of Legends champions, Vi and Jinx. Set in the fictional world of Runeterra, the series explores their journey through the corrupt and divided city of Piltover and the oppressed underground of Zaun. As they navigate through the complexities of their surroundings, they uncover deep-rooted secrets and face moral dilemmas that challenge their beliefs and values.
