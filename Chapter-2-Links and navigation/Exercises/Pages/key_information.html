Key Information

Release Date: November 6, 2021
Directors: Christian Linke, Alex Yee
Number of Seasons: 1
Number of Episodes: 9

Set in the vibrant universe of League of Legends, "Arcane" is a visually stunning animated series that premiered on November 6, 2021. Directed by Christian Linke and Alex Yee, the series consists of one season comprising nine episodes, each offering an immersive experience into the rich and diverse world of Runeterra.
